Milanesa

Description

Also named "Schnitzel" in other parts of the world, milanesas are a typical plate from your average Argentinian household. It basically consists of any kind of fried meat covered in bread crumbs but you can also add lemon, tomatoe, cheese, seasonings or combine it with french fries. Here, you will learn how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Meat, any kind. Beef is the most used.
  • Eggs.
  • Bread.
  • Garlic.
  • Parsley.
  • Cooking Oil.

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs and mix them with both the garlic and chopped parsley.
  2. Bath the meat with said mix.
  3. Completely cover the meat with breadcrumbs
  4. Save the milanesa inside the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
  5. Fry the milanesa in hot cooking oil until they look golden.
