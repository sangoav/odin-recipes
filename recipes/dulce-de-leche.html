Dulce de Leche

Description

Argentina's neighboring countries claim to be the inventors of this recipe and you may have even heard of it in Mexico named as "Cajeta". Honestly, I don't know who made it but if you do, do me a favour and go tell that man that he is a genius. This is a long recipe, tho. You better have some time in your hands if you think you are deserving of this dream made true.

Ingredients

  • Milk.
  • Sugar.
  • Baking Soda.
  • Vanilla extract.

Steps

  1. Heat up all the ingredients in a cooking pot while mixing constantly with a wooden spoon.
  2. Once the sugar gets dissolved, lower the fire a bit and keep mixing for 2 hours (told you). If the thing starts to boil, lower the fire and turn it up again once it cools down.
  3. Once it gets kind of thick, keep mixing constatly so it doesn't get lumpy.
  4. When you see that it gets a consistency similar to a custard, turn off the heat. Keep mixing until it cools down.
  5. Once it's cool, save it in jar and keep it in the refrigerator .
