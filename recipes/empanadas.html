Empanadas

Description

It is said that they have been brought to Argentina from the spanish during the colonization. However, some people also say that spanish have learnt this recipe from the arabs. The origin of this food is kind of unknown but no one can doubt that they are delicious.

Ingredients

  • Empanada Dough discs. You can buy them or you can make them yourself with flour, sugar, salt, butter and water.
  • Chopped Meat.
  • Onions.
  • Garlic.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Pepper.
  • Salt.
  • Cooking Oil.

Steps

  1. Chop an onion and a pepper and leave them in a heating cooking pot with oil until they are half cooked.
  2. Turn up the heat and add the chopped meat. Mix for some minutes. Then add chopped tomatoes and garlic and mix. Add salt to your liking. Cover the pot and leave it be until it's done.
  3. Turn off the heat and cool down the pot. Take the results to a pot and distribute it inside the dough discs.
  4. Close the dough so the empanadas have their iconical look and take them to the oven until they look golden.
